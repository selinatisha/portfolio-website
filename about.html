About me

Welcome to my website! My name is Selina, and I am a Junior Front-End Developer. On this page, you'll get to know me better and learn a bit about my passions and interests. Hopefully, this will help you form a picture of me and who I am!

My love for knitting/crocheting
I have a passion for knitting and crocheting that has been an interest of mine since I was a young teen. It all began with the gentle guidance of my beloved sister, who introduced me to the world of needles, yarns, and endless possibilities. For me, knitting and crocheting are not just hobbies; they are cherished forms of self-expression and artistic exploration. From the rhythmic click of the needles to the delicate dance of the crochet hook, each stitch carries with it a story, a sentiment, a piece of my soul intricately intertwined with every fiber. What I adore most about this craft is its ability to transport me into a realm of boundless creativity. Whether I'm crafting cozy scarves, intricate blankets, or whimsical amigurumi, every project is a canvas waiting to be adorned with my imagination.

World of wonders
As a web developer, my passion for crafting digital experiences is rooted in my lifelong love of fantasy books. From the young age when I first delved into the realms of magic and wonder, I've been captivated by the imaginative landscapes and enchanting characters that populate these pages. My journey into the world of fantasy began early. With each book I read, my imagination increases. Reading became more than just a hobby; it was a gateway to creativity and self-discovery. Through the pages of fantasy novels, I learned to envision new possibilities, to think outside the box, and to embrace my own unique perspective. As I honed my skills as a web developer, this love for creativity and imagination became ingrained in my approach to coding. Just as a fantasy author weaves together elements of magic and adventure to craft a compelling story, I harness the power of technology to bring digital dreams to life. So, while my passion for coding drives me to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm, it's my love for fantasy books that fuels my creativity and shapes who I am as a web developer.

The start of my coding journey
This passion for Web-Developement was ignited by my sister, who is a software developer herself. Her expertise, guidance, and unwavering support have been instrumental in shaping my path in the world of development. Before diving into web development, I used to work as a caregiver. As rewarding as it was, it just wasn't scratching that creative itch I had. So, I made the leap into coding. Coming from a background as a caregiver, I yearned for a career that would provide both intellectual stimulation and creative fulfillment. My transition from caregiving to web development was not without its challenges, but my passion for the craft and the unwavering support of my sister propelled me forward. Today, as I continue to evolve and grow in this dynamic field, I am grateful for the opportunity to pursue a career that fulfills me intellectually and allows me to make a meaningful impact through my work.
